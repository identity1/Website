Rocket

Go on Stick your head inside. Swirl, undulate, elevate The beat. Asteroids disperse, Starburst to the triggered Explosion. Explosion switches From direct to alternating— Implosion, explosion, implosion. When the milky bits Have reassembled The new beat Blows everything To pieces. Far away On a solitary Metal island, We watch With eager palms And fixated noses Pressed, jammed, smooshed, Into the thick round Of cheese-glass That separates us From the celestial rhythms Resonating wild, Outside.
